About Us

We're Building the Future of Student Portfolios

CASE was born from a simple idea — every student deserves a professional way to showcase their work, without the complexity of traditional hosting.

Our Mission

We believe talent shouldn't be hidden behind technical barriers. CASE — Code. Access. Share. Everywhere. — makes it effortless for students, freshers, and bootcamp graduates to showcase their projects and share a professional profile link with the world.

No complex setup. No technical barriers. Just add your project links, build your profile, and share — in under a minute.

Our Values

Simplicity First

We remove complexity so students can focus on what matters — building great projects.

Accessible to All

Free for students. No hidden costs, no premium gates, no barriers to entry.

Security & Trust

Your profile and project data are protected with industry-standard security and reliability.

The Team

CASE is built by a small team of developers and designers who are passionate about empowering the next generation of tech talent.
